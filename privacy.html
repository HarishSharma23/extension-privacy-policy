Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 25, 2025

Introduction

Auto Scroll & Page-Turn Capture (“the Extension”) is a Chrome browser extension that allows users to capture full-length webpages and page-turning eBooks by automatically scrolling or flipping through content and exporting it as a single image or PDF.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and what rights you have.

Information We Collect

  • We do not collect personally identifiable information.
  • We do not collect browsing history, financial data, or authentication details.
  • We only process the website content you explicitly choose to capture. This happens locally on your device.

How We Use Data

Captured content is used solely to generate the requested screenshot or PDF. No data is stored, transmitted, or sold to third parties.

Permissions Justification

  • activeTab & scripting: to scroll or flip pages and capture content.
  • storage: to save your extension preferences.
  • downloads: to save files to your device.
  • host permissions: to work on the sites you choose to capture.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share data with third parties. No analytics, tracking, or remote code is included.

Your Control

You control when and where to use the Extension. You can remove it anytime from Chrome.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will appear on this page.
